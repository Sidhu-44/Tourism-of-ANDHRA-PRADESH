Museums:

  1. Archaeological Museum at Amaravati Stupa --->> Features relics from nearby ancient sites
  2. Visakha Museum --->> Displays the history of the pre-Independence Madras Presidency
  3. Victoria Jubilee Museum --->> Has a collection of ancient sculptures, paintings, idols, weapons, cutlery, and inscriptions
  4. Bapu Museum --->> Has historical galleries, stone cut writings, coins, swords, body armor, shields, arms, and ornamentation
  5. Telugu Samskruthika Niketanam --->> Displays historical artefacts of the pre-independence era
