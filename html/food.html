Delicious Cuisine:

Andhra Pradesh is known for its spicy food, but it also has many sweet treats:

  • Chicken curry: A flavorful dish made with freshly ground masala and rich gravy.
  • Chicken Biryani: Andhra Pradesh’s Chicken Biryani is a flavorful, spiced dish made with marinated chicken and fragrant basmati rice, often cooked using the Dum method for rich taste.
  • Prawns curry: Prawns curry in Andhra Pradesh is a spicy and flavorful dish made with fresh prawns and a blend of aromatic spices.
  • Pulasa Fish Curry (Godavari district special): Pulasa fish, also known as Hilsa, is a prized delicacy in Andhra Pradesh, renowned for its rich flavor and tender texture, often cooked in spicy curries or served grilled.
  • Pulihora: A tamarind rice dish with a sour and salty taste made with curry leaves, tomatoes, and mustard seeds.
  • Punugulu: A flavorful street food snack made from urad dal and rice batter.
  • Bobbatlu: A sweet treat made with a filling of sweetened chana dal or coconut-jaggery mixture wrapped in dough.
  • Ulava charu: A soup made from horse gram.
  • Gongura mutton: A simple mutton curry cooked with gongura leaves that has an earthy, herbaceous taste.
  • Pesarattu: A crispy, protein-rich pancake made from ground green gram (moong dal) and eaten with spicy ginger chutney.
